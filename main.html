Hello and welcome ! I am Beatrice MBUAKI, founder of Bea Preeya, a space dedicated to the practice of meditation and gentle yoga. Always sensitive to spirituality, I discovered yoga at the age of 20 through a course offered by my university. Although this first lesson aroused my curiosity, I did not feel the need to practice diligently, too busy with my student life. However, ten years later, following a painful life episode, my life changed dramatically. Caught in a severe burnout, everything was collapsing around me. My salvation came in the form of an initiatory journey to India. I returned deeply moved. I was leaving France, my job and my loved ones to rebuild myself abroad. Once there, this time of forced introspection allowed me to discover Yin Yoga which I practice regularly. At the same time soft, stimulating and therapeutic, it reconnects me to my senses and especially my emotions. It is therefore natural that I registered for a yoga teacher training, with the idea of developing my knowledge. However, fate decided otherwise and the opportunity to teach did arise halfway through the training, I took great pleasure in it. This was how my adventure began and how Bea Preeya was born. Preeya being the name given to me during my travels in India. It means in Sanskrit: "the one who brings happiness" is the same meaning as my Latin name: a sign of destiny. Since 2019, I teach with passion and kindness, the practice of meditation, Yin and Hatha Yoga. My classes focus on grounding, breathing, letting go, acceptance and reconnecting to emotions. Both warm and inclusive, these sessions are a moment of sharing and communion. Everyone is welcome, regardless of their experience, background, gender, age, morphology, origins and beliefs. Beginner? I will be delighted to introduce you to this thousand-year-old and oh so transforming practice, whether during a group or private lesson. To find out more, do not hesitate to contact me and subscribe to the newsletter. You are one click away from positively changing your life ♡ -- Beatrice MBUAKI
